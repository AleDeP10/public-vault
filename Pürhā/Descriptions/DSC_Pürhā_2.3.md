# Concept 2.3 — Conceptual Minimalism + Floating Text

* **The Vibe:** Absolute silence, vastness, and a museum-installation feel. It’s the solitary monument left behind after the end of the world.
* **Colors & Light:** The background is an endless, borderless fog of pearl-gray and lavender-ash where sea and sky fuse. A single, dramatic, theatrical white spotlight cuts from above, isolating the hybrid instrument.
* **The Name "Pürhā":** Ethereal, milk-white letters evaporating softly into the fog above the object.
* **Why it's cool:** It strips away the chaos. Every crater of the rust and every charred fiber of the fusing wood becomes a sculpture. It forces the viewer to slow down and contemplate the relic in total silence.

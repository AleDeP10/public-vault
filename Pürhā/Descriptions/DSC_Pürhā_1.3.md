# Concept 1.3 — Hyper-Real Contrast + Carved Text

* **The Vibe:** Raw, aggressive, and hitting you right in the face. It's happening *right now* under a brutal tropical sun.
* **Colors & Light:** Blinding midday sunlight creating pitch-black, razor-sharp shadows. The ocean is an intense, almost electric ultramarine blue, and the palm trees are a vibrant neon green.
* **The Name "Pürhā":** Deeply and violently carved into the metal, with the sharp edges of the cuts catching bright white reflections like sunlit blades.
* **Why it's cool:** It doesn't hide behind vintage filters. It exposes every pore of the wood and every bleeding red crater of the rust. It's cool because it feels tense, urgent, and dangerously beautiful.

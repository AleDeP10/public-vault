# Concept 1.4 — Hyper-Real Contrast + Editorial Branding

* **The Vibe:** Ultra-modern, high-fashion, and striking. A bold collision of raw environmental destruction and high-end graphic design.
* **Colors & Light:** The same unforgiving, saturated palette of the 1.3 variation—deep bleeding rust, piercing blue waters, and harsh lighting.
* **The Name "Pürhā":** A crisp, pure white, minimalist designer logo stamped over the chaotic, violent landscape.
* **Why it's cool:** It creates an amazing aesthetic clash. It tells the viewer that despite the chaotic, overwhelming heat and historical weight of the scene, the artist's brand dominates the frame with absolute calm, sharp precision, and power.

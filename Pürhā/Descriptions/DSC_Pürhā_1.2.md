# Concept 1.2 — Nostalgic Warmth + Editorial Branding

* **The Vibe:** Elegant, cinematic, and curated. It feels like a premium print inside a high-end photography magazine or a gallery exhibition.
* **Colors & Light:** Shares the same warm, sun-drenched, analog seppia tones and golden grain as version 1.1.
* **The Name "Pürhā":** Printed cleanly in a razor-thin, wide-spaced elegant serif font in the corner of the cover.
* **Why it's cool:** The contrast is key here. By placing a beautiful, clean modern layout over a dusty, decayed historical scene, the image instantly looks sophisticated and intellectual. It transforms a simple photo into a deliberate piece of modern art.

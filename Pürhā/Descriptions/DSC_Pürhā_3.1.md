# Concept 3.1 — Minimalist Graphic / Pastel Tones + Integrated Text

* **The Vibe:** Quiet luxury, Japanese and Scandinavian minimalist design. It looks like an illustration from an independent cultural magazine.
* **Colors & Style:** A highly restrained palette of flat, desaturated tones: dusty sage green, pale powder pink, faded ochre, and Nordic gray-blue. Zero textures, zero gradients—pure vector-like shapes.
* **The Subject:** The cannon is reduced to a simple terracotta-colored cylinder; the guitar is a clean geometric "8" silhouette. Tropical palms are transformed into abstract arches.
* **Why it's cool:** It uses intellectual irony. Treating a weapon of war and a musical instrument with the exact same delicate pastel grace domesticates the violent history through design. It makes the subject harmless, playful, and incredibly chic for merchandise.

# Concept 1.1 — Nostalgic Warmth + Carved Text

* **The Vibe:** Warm, analog, and nostalgic. It feels like a long-lost photograph from the 1970s found in an old drawer.
* **Colors & Light:** Bathed in a rich golden hour sunlight. The guitar wood glows like warm honey, while the cannon's rust turns into a deep, earthy terracotta orange against a soft petrol-blue ocean.
* **The Name "Pürhā":** Scratched directly and imperfectly into the rusty metal barrel, looking like an old soldier's graffiti.
* **Why it's cool:** It whispers rather than shouts. It makes the heavy theme of war feel romanticized by time, showing that music ultimately colonized the weapon and turned it into an artifact of memory.

# Concept 2.2 — Psychedelic Hallucination + Hologram Text

* **The Vibe:** Tropical sci-fi and botanical cyberpunk. Think "Blade Runner meets Avatar" applied to a historical, musical relic.
* **Colors & Light:** Shares the same neon-purple night, glowing watercolor ocean, and internal amber guitar luminescence as 2.1.
* **The Name "Pürhā":** A weak, glowing holographic projection. Sharp, geometric, electric-blue sans-serif text with a subtle scan-line/glitch effect stamped right where the metal meets the wood.
* **Why it's cool:** It creates a time loop. The past (colonial cannon), present (mutating nature), and future (glitch hologram) collide. It feels like a transmission from a superior intelligence or a futuristic certificate of authenticity.

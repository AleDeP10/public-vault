# Concept 2.1 — Psychedelic Hallucination + Floating Text

* **The Vibe:** An impossible tropical night, hypnotizing and lisergic. It feels like a 1970s prog-rock album cover re-imagined for a modern audience.
* **Colors & Light:** A deep indigo and magenta sky with a glowing neon-cyan ocean. The guitar doesn't receive light—it glows from within like an amber lantern, casting long, liquid-purple shadows.
* **The Name "Pürhā":** Floating and curving in the air, emerging from the cannon like bioluminescent, UV-light smoke or a crystallized musical echo.
* **Why it's cool:** The cannon is morphing into nature, with piano keys and guitar-string vines growing out of the rust. It’s cool because the music literally rewrites the laws of physics and history, digesting the weapon of war.

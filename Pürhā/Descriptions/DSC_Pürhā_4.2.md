# Concept 4.2 — Macro Material Detail + Technical Hologram

* **The Vibe:** "Secret Archive" and high-tech found-footage aesthetic. It feels like a rare, centuries-old relic being scanned by a futuristic laboratory.
* **Colors & Textures:** Same hyper-detailed, planetary-scale rust craters and glowing translucent string under a sharp, dramatic side-light.
* **The Typography:** The name "Pürhā" changes from atmospheric dust into a surgical inscription. It is projected as a razor-thin, ultra-bright laser blueprint (blinding pure white or neon red) directly onto an ignored, shadowed crease of the stone or metal.
* **Why it's cool:** It forces two impossibly distant timelines to coexist: ancient, decaying, colonial iron and a clinical, sci-fi marking device. The music of Pürhā becomes an artifact that a future civilization is studying with archaeological fascination.

# Concept 3.4 — Abstract Expressionism / Sharp Contrasts + Editorial Anchor

* **The Vibe:** High-concept contemporary art gallery poster. A deliberate visual paradox that balances rebellious avant-garde energy with institutional order.
* **Colors & Style:** Features the exact same chaotic, high-contrast abstract collage of red, cobalt, yellow, and black from variant 3.3.
* **The Typography:** A literal "window of silence." A clean, solid white or black rectangular safe-zone cuts into the chaos, housing the name "Pürhā" in a neutral, institutional font (like Helvetica Neue). It behaves like a clean museum caption next to a punk masterpiece.
* **Why it's cool:** It creates a powerful graphic short-circuit. The wild, aggressive energy of the abstract painting is suddenly "certified" by a clinical, bureaucratic block of text. It radiates ultimate artistic control: the artist knows exactly how to command attention even when everything is exploding.

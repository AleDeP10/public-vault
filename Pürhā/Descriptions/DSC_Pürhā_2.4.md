# Concept 2.4 — Conceptual Minimalism + Minimal Branding

* **The Vibe:** Cold, sophisticated, and editorial. Inspired by high-end Japanese art photography and minimalist ambient label designs (like ECM Records).
* **Colors & Light:** The same endless, melancholic gray-blue mist and dramatic vertical spotlight as 2.3, sealing the object in an invisible glass display case.
* **The Name "Pürhā":** Tiny, razor-thin, geometric text tucked away or faintly engraved as a thin line of cold light following the curve of the iron.
* **Why it's cool:** It’s the ultimate "less is more" statement. It doesn't scream for attention, radiating absolute confidence. The music has traversed the war, passed through the psychedelic madness, and finally arrived at a ritual, essential peace.

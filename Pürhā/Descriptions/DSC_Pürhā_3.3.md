# Concept 3.3 — Abstract Expressionism / Sharp Contrasts + Integrated Text

* **The Vibe:** High-energy modernism, reminiscent of early 20th-century avant-garde art or a dynamic concert poster. It's an adrenaline shot.
* **Colors & Style:** Aggressive, fully saturated flat colors clashing directly: lacquer red, deep cobalt blue, chrome yellow, and absolute ink-black. Hard, sharp edges cut like a blade with zero smoothing.
* **The Subject:** Complete fragmentation. The objects are smashed into abstract geometric vectors (Depero/Delaunay style). The guitar neck becomes a black diagonal blade; the cannon breaks into misaligned blocks; the strings vibrate like yellow lightning bolts.
* **Why it's cool:** The typography itself is shattered and scattered across the composition, forcing the viewer's eye to move. It communicates a powerful message: music literally disarms war by breaking it down into a playful explosion of shapes and colors.

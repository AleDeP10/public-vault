A perfect 1:1 square aspect ratio album cover design. Minimalist 2D vector illustration style, flat graphic art, screenprint aesthetic. The composition features a highly stylized, abstract geometric silhouette of a classical acoustic guitar and a simplified cylinder representing a colonial cannon, integrated with abstract curved arches of palm leaves. The color palette is strictly limited to flat, muted, desaturated pastel tones: dusty sage green, pale powder pink, faded ochre yellow, and a soft Nordic gray-blue background. No textures, no gradients, clean solid color fields. The artist name "Pürhā" is beautifully integrated into the graphic layout, written in a clean, rounded minimalist geometric sans-serif font, smoothly following the curve of the geometric shapes as part of the illustration.

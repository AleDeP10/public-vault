A perfect 1:1 square aspect ratio photographic album cover composition. A classical acoustic guitar leans against a massive, deeply corroded colonial iron cannon on an ancient stone wall. Background features a vibrant, piercing ultramarine blue tropical ocean and hyper-saturated green palm trees under a brutal, unforgiving noon sun. High contrast, sharp shadows, extreme material textures of bleeding red rust and detailed wood grain. In the top-left corner, contrasting with the chaotic, violent hyper-real scene, the artist name "Pürhā" is printed in a minimalist, razor-thin, elegant serif font. The text is crisp pure white, perfectly aligned, looking like a contemporary high-end designer logo stamped on a battlefield. Modern, sophisticated, high-fashion contrast between raw destruction and clean graphic order.

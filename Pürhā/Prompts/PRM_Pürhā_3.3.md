A perfect 1:1 square aspect ratio album cover design. Avant-garde abstract expressionism, bold modernism collage style inspired by Fortunato Depero. High-energy dynamic composition with sharp diagonal cuts and hard edges. The shapes of a classical guitar and a colonial cannon are completely fragmented and shattered into abstract geometric vectors: sharp triangles, skewed trapezoids, and jagged lines. The color palette is violently saturated with flat fields of intense lacquer red, deep cobalt blue, vibrant chrome yellow, and absolute solid black. The artist name "Pürhā" is broken apart and scattered across the composition, with individual letters integrated dynamically into the clashing geometric color blocks, creating a sense of a visual explosion.

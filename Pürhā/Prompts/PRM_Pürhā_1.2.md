A perfect 1:1 square aspect ratio photographic album cover composition. A classical acoustic guitar is leaning against a massive, heavily rusted colonial iron cannon on an ancient stone rampart overlooking a calm tropical ocean. Lush palm trees and tropical greenery in the background. Nostalgic 1970s analog film aesthetic, light film grain, soft vignetting. Warm golden hour lighting, amber and seppia color palette, glowing wooden textures on the guitar and deep terracotta rust on the cannon. In the bottom-right corner, the artist name "Pürhā" is cleanly printed in a very thin, elegant serif font, with wide letter-spacing (tracking), ivory-white color, slightly translucent, looking like a professional photography magazine layout. Editorial, curated gallery exhibition aesthetic, balance between gritty history and refined graphic design.

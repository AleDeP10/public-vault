A perfect 1:1 square aspect ratio album cover design. Contemporary art gallery poster style. The main area features a chaotic, high-contrast abstract modernist collage of a fragmented guitar and cannon, composed of sharp geometric shapes in flat lacquer red, cobalt blue, and solid black. Breaking this chaotic pattern, a clean, solid, rectangular white safe-zone box is placed precisely at the bottom. Inside this white box, the artist name "Pürhā" is printed in a clinical, ultra-clean, neutral institutional sans-serif font, perfectly centered. A stark visual short-circuit between the rebellious energy of abstract art and the absolute order of institutional typography.

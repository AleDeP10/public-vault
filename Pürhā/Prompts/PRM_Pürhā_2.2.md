A perfect 1:1 square aspect ratio photographic album cover composition. A tropical sci-fi cyberpunk scene at night. A hybrid surreal object of a classical acoustic guitar seamlessly fused with a deeply rusted colonial cannon, with glowing green vines and piano keys growing out of the iron cracks. The environment is saturated with an electric purple sky and a glowing turquoise ocean. The guitar glows from within with a warm orange light. The artist name "Pürhā" is displayed as a weak holographic projection directly onto the hybrid cannon barrel. The text is in a sharp, minimalist, geometric sans-serif font, glowing in a cold electric-blue color with subtle sci-fi scan-lines and a light digital glitch effect. Botanical cyberpunk aesthetic, contrast between organic hallucination and futuristic technology.

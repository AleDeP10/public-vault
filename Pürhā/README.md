# Pürhā — Album Cover Art Development Roadmap

This workspace tracks the progressive refinement and collaborative creation of the artwork for Pürhā's upcoming album. The central artistic theme explores the sharp contrast and emotional fusion between **Music and War within a Tropical Environment**.

Our primary goal is to develop a series of high-quality Proof of Concepts (PoC) to submit for final production.

---

## 🛠️ The Collaborative AI Workflow

To ensure maximum creative control and efficiency, we use a hybrid multi-AI pipeline:
*   **Gemini (Primary Organizer & Architect):** Maps out the overarching visual concepts, manages the file vault structures, and engineers the precise technical prompt syntax.
*   **Claude (Artistic & Emotional Director):** Deep-dives into the psychological aspects of the artwork, fine-tuning the color theory, lighting conditions, textures, and specific emotional impacts.
*   **Flux (Rapid Prototyping):** Used to generate immediate "raw previews" (PRV) to test prompt mechanics and textual alignment.
*   **Leroy (Final Production Asset):** The winning prompt approved by Vivien will be handed over to Leroy for premium, high-resolution rendering and final mastering.

---

## 🗺️ Conceptual Directions & Versioning

We have developed 5 distinct conceptual directions. While the first four concepts balance 4 specific variations, the final concept has been extended to 5 iterations to capture Martina's definitive cathartic vision, for a total of 21 unique iterations.

### Folder Structure & Naming Convention
*   `/Prompts/PRM_Pürhā_X.Y.md` — The technical code submitted to the generator.
*   `/Descriptions/DSC_Pürhā_X.Y.md` — A high-level emotional summary for creative review.
*   `/Previews/PRV_Pürhā_X.Y.png` — The visual prototype generated during testing.

*(Where **X** is the Core Concept [1 to 5] and **Y** is the Styling Variation [1 to 5])*
